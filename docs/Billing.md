# Billing

## Create invoice
To create an invoice, go to Billing → Invoices → New invoice, add line items,
then click Send. Invoices can be exported as PDF and are sent to the customer email.

## Payment methods
We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard) and bank transfer.
Bank transfer invoices are marked as paid manually after the funds arrive.

## Refunds
Refunds can be issued within 60 days from the Billing → Payments screen.
A refund returns the full or partial amount to the original payment method.
